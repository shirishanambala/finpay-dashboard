Settlement

Your money, on your schedule

Transparent settlement with no hidden deductions, real-time tracking, and flexible payout schedules to match your cash flow needs.

  1. 01

    Customer pays

    Payment is authorised, processed, and confirmed in real time. Your FinPay dashboard updates instantly.

  2. →
  3. 02

    Funds held securely

    Collected funds are held in a segregated, regulated escrow account while fraud checks and reconciliation complete.

  4. →
  5. 03

    Settlement initiated

    On your chosen schedule — same day, next day, or T+2 — FinPay batches and initiates the transfer to your bank account.

  6. →
  7. 04

    Funds in your account

    Money arrives in your bank account. A full settlement report is available in your dashboard and via the Settlements API.

  • Same-Day Settlement

    Available for eligible businesses. Funds reach your account the same day payments are collected.

  • Next-Day Settlement

    The FinPay standard. Payments collected today are in your bank account by 9 AM the next business day.

    Standard

  • Custom Schedule

    Enterprise customers can configure custom settlement windows — weekly, bi-weekly, or on-demand via API.

Security & Compliance

Security isn't a feature. It's the foundation.

Every payment processed through FinPay is protected by the highest security standards in the industry — active by default, with no configuration required.

  • PCI DSS Level 1

    The highest tier of payment card security certification — independently audited annually. Your card data never touches your servers.

  • AES-256 Encryption

    All sensitive data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3. Payment tokens replace raw card data throughout.

  • 3D Secure 2.0

    Frictionless authentication for low-risk transactions, step-up challenge only when needed — maximising conversion while minimising fraud.

  • ML Fraud Detection

    Our machine-learning fraud engine analyses 200+ signals per transaction in real time, blocking fraud before it reaches your customers.

  • Tokenisation

    Card numbers are replaced with secure tokens at the point of entry — stored tokens can be re-used for one-click and subscription payments.

  • 24/7 Monitoring

    Round-the-clock infrastructure monitoring, automated incident response, and a dedicated security team that never sleeps.
