Introduction

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember your actions and preferences over time, so you don't have to re-enter them every time you visit or navigate between pages.

FinPay uses cookies and similar technologies (such as local storage and session storage) to provide, protect, and improve our platform. We never use cookies to identify you personally without your consent.

📁

Session Cookies

Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser. Used for things like keeping you logged in during a session.

💾

Persistent Cookies

Stored on your device for a set period. Used to remember preferences across visits.

🏠

First-Party Cookies

Set directly by FinPay for core functionality and performance measurement.

🌐

Third-Party Cookies

Set by our trusted partners for analytics and, where you've consented, personalised advertising.

Cookie Categories

The four types we use

We categorise every cookie we place into one of four groups. Essential cookies are always active. You can freely manage the others in the preference panel below.

🔒

Essential

Required for the platform to function. Cannot be turned off — but they don't store any personal data.

📊

Analytics

Help us understand how people use FinPay so we can improve it. All data is anonymised and aggregated.

🎯

Marketing

Used to show relevant advertising on third-party platforms. Only active with your explicit consent.

⭐

Personalisation

Remember your preferences like theme and language to give you a consistent, tailored experience.

ℹ️
GDPR & ePrivacy compliant We obtain verifiable consent before setting any non-essential cookies, in line with GDPR Article 7 and the EU ePrivacy Directive. You can withdraw consent at any time using the panel below.

Marketing Cookies

Relevant ads — only with consent

Marketing cookies are only set after you give explicit consent. They allow our advertising partners to show you FinPay-related content on third-party platforms. We never sell your data to advertisers.

⚠️
Consent required Marketing cookies are turned off by default. You must actively enable them in the preference panel below. You can change your mind at any time.
