Payment Card Industry

PCI DSS Level 1 — the highest tier

FinPay is a PCI DSS Level 1 certified service provider — the highest designation in the payment card industry. Our infrastructure is assessed annually by a Qualified Security Assessor (QSA) and we maintain continuous compliance through automated control monitoring.

💳
PCI DSS v4.0
Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard
Active

Covers all 12 PCI DSS requirements across our cardholder data environment, including network security, encryption, access control, and monitoring.

  • Annual on-site assessment by accredited QSA
  • Quarterly network vulnerability scans
  • Card data handled exclusively within tokenised vault
  • Full cardholder data environment (CDE) scoping
  • Annual penetration testing of CDE systems
Level 1 · Assessed 2025 · Next audit Q1 2026 Request AoC →
🔒

Tokenisation

Raw card numbers are never stored. All PANs are replaced with tokens at the point of entry into our systems.

🌐

Network Segmentation

The CDE is fully isolated from other network segments using firewalls, VPCs, and strict access control lists.

👁️

Continuous Monitoring

All access to cardholder data environments is logged, monitored, and alerted on 24/7 by our SOC team.

Data Protection

GDPR — privacy by design

FinPay is fully compliant with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the UK GDPR post-Brexit. We act as both a data controller and data processor depending on context, and maintain appropriate legal bases for all personal data processing activities.

🇪🇺
EU GDPR
General Data Protection Regulation (2016/679)
Compliant

Our GDPR programme covers lawful basis documentation, data subject rights fulfilment, DPA agreements, and cross-border transfer mechanisms.

  • Data Protection Officer (DPO) appointed
  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for data transfers
  • Records of Processing Activities (RoPA) maintained
  • Data Subject Access Requests fulfilled within 30 days
  • Privacy by design embedded in product development
EU & UK GDPR · DPO appointed · RoPA current Privacy Policy →

Data Processing Agreements All merchants processing personal data through FinPay must sign a Data Processing Agreement (DPA). Enterprise customers can download our standard DPA from the compliance portal or request a custom version via their account manager.

Information Security Management

ISO 27001 — certified since 2021

ISO/IEC 27001:2022 is the international gold standard for information security management systems (ISMS). FinPay has been certified since 2021 and maintains surveillance audits every six months with a full recertification every three years.

🌐
ISO/IEC 27001:2022
Information Security Management System
Certified

Our ISMS covers the full scope of FinPay's payment processing platform, APIs, cloud infrastructure, and corporate IT systems.

  • 93 Annex A controls implemented and verified
  • Statement of Applicability (SoA) published internally
  • Risk treatment plan reviewed semi-annually
  • Surveillance audits every 6 months
  • Internal audit programme across all departments

Financial Crime Prevention

AML / CFT compliance

FinPay maintains a comprehensive Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Counter-Financing of Terrorism (CFT) programme that meets the requirements of the Financial Action Task Force (FATF) recommendations and the EU's Anti-Money Laundering Directives (AMLD).

🚨

Transaction Monitoring

Automated rules and ML models monitor all transactions for patterns indicative of money laundering or sanctions evasion.

📋

Suspicious Activity Reports

Dedicated MLRO and compliance team reviews alerts and files SARs with the relevant Financial Intelligence Unit within required timeframes.

🌍

Sanctions Screening

All customers and transactions are screened against OFAC, EU, UN, and HM Treasury sanctions lists in real time.

🔄

Programme Reviews

Our AML programme undergoes annual independent review by external legal counsel specialising in financial crime.

Identity Verification

KYC — frictionless, not reckless

Our Know Your Customer programme combines regulated identity checks with a streamlined user experience, so onboarding is fast for legitimate businesses without compromising the rigour regulators require.

🪪

Identity Verification

Document authentication and biometric liveness checks using IDEMIA and Onfido for individuals and directors.

🏢

Business Verification

Corporate registry lookups, beneficial ownership mapping to 25%+ threshold, and UBO verification across 180+ jurisdictions.

⚡

Instant Onboarding

Risk-tiered onboarding means low-risk merchants can start transacting in minutes while enhanced due diligence runs asynchronously.

🔄

Ongoing Monitoring

Customer risk ratings are recalculated automatically when adverse media, PEP status, or transaction behaviour changes.

ℹ️
KYC as a Service Platform merchants can leverage FinPay's KYC infrastructure to verify their own end customers. Our KYC API supports 195+ countries, 10,000+ document types, and returns a decision in under 60 seconds.
