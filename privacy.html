Your Privacy Matters

Introduction

FinPay Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("FinPay", "we", "our", "us") operates a global payment platform accessible via finpay.io and associated mobile applications. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information when you use our services, visit our website, or interact with our team.

This policy applies to all users of FinPay's products worldwide. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), additional rights apply under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), described in the Your Rights section.

💡
Plain English summary: We collect only what we need to provide our service, we never sell your data, and we give you full control over what you share with us.
🔒 GDPR Compliant
✅ No Data Selling
🌏 ISO 27001 Certified
🛡 SOC 2 Type II
📄

Data Collection

We collect information in three ways: data you provide directly, data generated automatically when you use our services, and data we receive from trusted third parties.

👤

Account Information

Name, email, phone number, business details, and government-issued ID for KYC verification.

💳

Payment Data

Transaction amounts, timestamps, merchant details. Card numbers are tokenised — we never store raw card data.

📊

Usage Analytics

Pages visited, features used, session duration, device type, and IP address to improve our product.

📞

Communications

Messages you send to our support team, survey responses, and feedback you submit.

How We Use Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes, always tied to a clear legal basis under applicable privacy law:

  • ⚡

    Providing the Service

    Processing payments, managing your account, sending transaction confirmations, and providing customer support.

  • 🛡

    Fraud Prevention & Security

    Detecting suspicious activity, preventing unauthorised access, and complying with anti-money-laundering regulations.

  • 📈

    Product Improvement

    Analysing how features are used so we can fix bugs, improve performance, and build better products.

  • 💬

    Communications

    Sending product updates, policy changes, and (with your consent) marketing emails about features relevant to your business.

🍪

Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to keep you signed in, understand how you use our platform, and occasionally show you relevant promotions. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via our Cookie Settings page.

✅
Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for the site to function. All other cookies are optional and require your consent.
🌐

Third-Party Services

We work with carefully vetted third-party providers to deliver parts of our service. These partners are bound by data processing agreements and may only use your data as directed by FinPay.

Our key processors include: AWS (cloud infrastructure), Stripe (card network connectivity), Twilio (SMS notifications), Intercom (customer support), and Google Analytics (anonymised usage analytics). We never sell your personal data to third parties for advertising purposes.

🛠

Data Protection

Protecting your data is built into every layer of our platform, not bolted on as an afterthought.

🔐

Encryption at Rest

All stored data is encrypted using AES-256. Database backups are encrypted with separate key management.

🔑

Encryption in Transit

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers uses TLS 1.3 with perfect forward secrecy.

📋

Access Controls

Role-based access control (RBAC) limits internal access. Engineers require MFA and VPN for all production systems.

👨‍⚖

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have the following rights regarding your personal data. To exercise any right, email privacy@finpay.io or use the controls in your account settings.

  • 🔍

    Right to Access

    Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you, delivered within 30 days.

  • 🖉

    Right to Rectification

    Correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data directly in your account settings.

  • 🗑

    Right to Erasure

    Request deletion of your data where we have no legal obligation to retain it.

  • 🛍

    Right to Portability

    Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON or CSV).

  • 🚫

    Right to Object

    Object to processing based on legitimate interests, including direct marketing.
