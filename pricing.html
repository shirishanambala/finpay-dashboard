Frequently asked questions

Have a question not answered below? Talk to our team.

Are there any setup or hidden fees?
No. FinPay charges only the platform fee (if applicable on your plan) and per-transaction fees. There are no setup fees, no monthly minimums on the Starter plan, and no hidden charges. GST is applicable on all fees as per Indian tax regulations.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your FinPay dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle. You'll be credited any unused portion of your current plan when upgrading.
How does settlement work?
Funds collected via FinPay are settled directly to your registered bank account. Standard settlement timelines are T+1 for most methods and T+2 for net banking. Enterprise customers can negotiate same-day settlement as part of their agreement.
What is the GMV limit on the Starter plan?
The Starter plan supports up to ₹5 lakh in Gross Merchandise Value per month. If your volume consistently exceeds this, we'll reach out to help you move to the Growth plan — your payments will never be interrupted.
Is GST included in the listed fees?
No. All stated fees are exclusive of GST. Applicable GST at 18% is added to platform subscription fees and transaction fees. A tax invoice is issued for every billing period.
Does FinPay support international payments on all plans?
International payment acceptance is available on the Scale plan and above. If you're on Growth and need to accept a foreign currency payment, our team can activate international payments as an add-on. Contact us for details.
What payment methods do you support?
FinPay supports 100+ payment methods including Visa, Mastercard, Rupay, UPI (all apps), IMPS/NEFT/RTGS, net banking (60+ banks), popular digital wallets, EMI on credit cards, and Buy Now Pay Later. International plans additionally support PayPal, Alipay, and major foreign card networks.
How do I get started?
Create a free account, complete KYC verification, and integrate FinPay into your website or app using our API or no-code tools. You can begin accepting live payments within one business day of completing verification.
